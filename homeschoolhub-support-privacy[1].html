HomeschoolHub Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2026

This privacy policy describes how HomeschoolHub ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when using HomeschoolHub:

  • Account Information: Email address for account creation and authentication
  • Profile Information: Parent and student names, grade levels, profile photos
  • Educational Data: Lesson information (titles, subjects, notes, dates, completion status)
  • Media: Photos you choose to upload for lessons or profiles
  • Subscription Information: Purchase history and subscription status (processed through Apple)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide app functionality and features
  • Save and sync your data across your devices
  • Process subscription payments through Apple's in-app purchase system
  • Improve the app experience and fix bugs
  • Respond to your support requests

Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services with industry-standard encryption
  • Apple iCloud: Optional backup through your Apple account

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption at rest and in transit to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database hosting and user authentication
  • Apple App Store: Payment processing for subscriptions (we do not store payment information)

We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any third parties for marketing purposes.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not:

  • Track your activity across other apps or websites
  • Share your data with advertising networks
  • Sell your information to third parties
  • Use your data for purposes unrelated to providing the app's functionality

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your data anytime within the app
  • Edit: Modify your information at any time
  • Delete: Delete your account and all associated data permanently
  • Export: Request a copy of your data by contacting us
  • Contact Us: Ask questions about your privacy and data

Children's Privacy

HomeschoolHub is designed for parents and educators. While the app is used to track educational information about children, we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. All accounts must be created and managed by adults (parents or legal guardians).

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in servers located in different countries. We ensure all data transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
